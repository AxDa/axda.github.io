Current Context: King Charles in Bermuda

King Charles is currently on a high‑profile visit to Bermuda, where multiple reputable news outlets report he is carrying out a three‑day royal tour following his U.S. state visit.

His Bermuda trip marks his first visit to a British Overseas Territory as monarch, and the first time in 400 years that a reigning king has visited the islands. Reports describe a full ceremonial welcome, key engagements in defence and space science, cultural and historical events, and a warm public reception across the island.
